ABOUT
(Türkçe için buraya tıklayın)

SmarDish is an ingredient based recipe finder, mostly focusing on Greek - Turkish cuisines for a Mediterranean diet. You select what you already have as vegetables, legumes, dairy products etc. and our system will provide you with our suggestions to prepare or cook.

All our content is crafted by our editors, checked and updated regularly. You can save your favorite recipes, and in case you have a few missing ingredients, you can add them into your shopping list.

We are a small team of food enthusiasts, and we are always open to your feedback and suggestions. Please feel free to contact us for any inquiries.
