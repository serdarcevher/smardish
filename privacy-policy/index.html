This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the SmarDish app running on any device and platform.

Information we collect

"SmarDish" is sub-brand of our ingredient based Turkish & Mediterranean recipes finding engine: "Tarifmotoru", which is located in its respective web address (https://tarifmotoru.com).

You should create a user account to use the SmarDish app.

While using our Service, we require you to provide us with certain information about you and your kitchen, such as:

  • Your name and your email address,
  • Your existing ingredients in your fridge,
  • Your potential future ingredients (your shopping list),
  • Your favorite meals

Technical basics

The information you provide prior to getting your meal recipe suggestions is sent to our servers to be processed and to be the base while generating the list for you.

Our server software may also store basic technical information, such as your IP address and your device's operating system, in temporary memory or logs.

Your email, your name, your fridge ingredients, your shopist and your favorite meals are saved in our servers. You always have the chance to delete your account and this will wipe all your entries in our database.

We are using Google Analytics to collect traffic analysis related data. We are using Google Admob to monetize our app with ads.
